What is TopoTrail?

TopoTrail is the easiest way to see and plan outdoor adventures overlayed on real-world terrain.

At TopoTrail you can search from trails shared by others, or add your own by uploading GPS trail files in common formats (currently supports .GPX and .GEOJSON). Just type a name and description, upload a file, and we'll do all the hard work!

Going on a hike and want to know if there are hazardous drop-offs along the trail? We'll show you!

Planning a bike ride and want to see how tired your legs will be at the end? You'll see your future pain in 3D!

Leading a climb and want to visualize the ascent for your group? There's never been a better way to see it!

Take TopoTrail for a spin!
