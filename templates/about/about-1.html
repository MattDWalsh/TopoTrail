TopoTrail

What is TopoTrail?

  • TopoTrail allows users to view hiking, climbing, and cycling "trails" overlayed on 3D terrain
  • Users can upload their own trail files and TopoTrail will generate this content for them
  • Trails can have photos and comments added to them, where additional trail details can be augmented
